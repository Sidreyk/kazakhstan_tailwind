About Kazakhstan

Discover the rich heritage, diverse landscape, and unique cultural aspects of Central Asia's largest country.

Geography

Kazakhstan is the world's largest landlocked country and the ninth largest country overall.

  • Borders: Russia, China, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, Turkmenistan
  • Terrain: Steppes, deserts, mountains, lakes
  • Major Mountain Ranges: Tian Shan, Altai

Languages

  • Kazakh (State Language)
  • Russian (Official Language)
  • Other regional languages

Culture

  • Rich nomadic heritage
  • Traditional arts and music
  • Famous hospitality
  • Diverse ethnic influences
